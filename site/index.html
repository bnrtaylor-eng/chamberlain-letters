Welcome! This site is a scholarly digital edition of The Letters of John Chamberlain, offering TEI-encoded transcriptions of Chamberlain’s correspondence from early seventeenth-century England.

The edition allows users to browse letters, explore referenced people, places, and organizations, and read editorial notes contextualizing each letter's historical moment. All content is licensed under CC BY 4.0 and contributed by a growing community of scholars, students, and editors.
